Welcome to our D&D Club!

We are a community of passionate role-players who gather to embark on epic adventures in the world of Dungeons & Dragons. Whether you're a seasoned veteran or a newcomer to the game, our club offers a welcoming and inclusive space for everyone to explore the realms of imagination, strategy, and camaraderie.

Join us as we delve into dungeons, battle fearsome monsters, uncover ancient mysteries, and forge unforgettable stories together. Our club meets regularly to play campaigns, share tips and tricks, and celebrate the creativity and teamwork that make D&D such a beloved game.

Come discover new worlds, make new friends, and experience the thrill of crafting your own heroic saga. The dice are calling, so grab your character sheet and embark on an adventure with us!

Club Rules

  1. Be kind to others. This is a safe space and we expect everyone to be civil and respect each other.
  2. The DM's word is final. If you have any issues with a ruling, speak to them afterwords so games don't get de-railed over rule discrepancies.
  3. Keep conversations during games to a minimum. It's great that people are getting along and socialising but everyone else shoudln't be held up waiting for you to finish your conversation. That's what "Social Saturdays" are for!
